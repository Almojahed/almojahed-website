Who We Are

A multi-activity company built for Sudan's factory owners

Established in 2024, Almojahed Expansion Company Ltd. is a multi-activity limited company headquartered in Kassala, Sudan, with a dedicated showroom in the heart of Khartoum. We specialize in the supply of Low Voltage electrical materials, renewable solar energy solutions, and complete industrial services — making us the single trusted partner for factory owners across Sudan.

Our in-house fleet of more than five trucks lets us deliver materials and equipment directly to your site, on schedule, every time. Whether you need a single transformer or a full factory electrical fit-out, Almojahed has the stock, the engineering team, and the logistics to deliver.

Our Services

One accountable contract, start to finish

Design, supply, installation and maintenance — our multi-disciplinary team covers every stage of your project on a single circuit of responsibility.

LV Electrical Supply & Distribution التوريد والتوزيع

Complete supply of all LV materials — cables, switchgear, MCBs, MCCBs, sockets, transformers and accessories. Same-day dispatch from our Khartoum showroom; bulk orders for factories and contractors welcome.

Solar & Renewable Energy الطاقة الشمسية

Design, supply and commissioning of solar PV systems for industrial and commercial facilities — inverters, batteries, panels, mounting and grid-tie. Full warranty and after-sales support.

Installation & Maintenance التركيب والصيانة

Professional installation of LV panels, distribution boards, lighting, earthing and cable management. Preventive and corrective maintenance contracts for factories.

Factory Fit-Out & Turnkey مشاريع جاهزة

Turnkey LV electrical fit-out for new factories and expansions — from design and BOQ through installation, testing, commissioning and handover.

Fleet Logistics & Delivery اللوجستيات والتوصيل

More than five owned trucks for heavy material transport. Scheduled, reliable delivery of equipment, cables and solar systems to factory sites across Sudan.

Khartoum Showroom & Technical Sales المعرض والمبيعات
